# 1-Tableau-Data-Visualization-Project-HR-Analysis-

## 📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates **interactive dashboards, storytelling, and actionable insights using Tableau**. The maim aim of the project is to generate insights and make an super interactive HR dashboard. 

**Disclaimer:** Data is for educational/portfolio purposes only.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Tableau Desktop / Tableau Public  
- Source Data: Excel / CSV  
- Tableau Features: Dashboards, Filters, Parameters, Calculated Fields, Interactive Visuals  
- Data Analysis & Visualization

---

## ❓ Business Questions
1. Display the total number of Hired People, Active & Terminated People.
2. Visualise the total number of of hired and terminated employees over the years.
3. Present a breakdown of total employees by department and job titles.
4. Compare total employees betweeen Headquarters (HQ) and brances ( New York is HQ ).
5. Show the distribution of employees by city and state.
6. Present the gender ratio in the company.
7. Visualize the distribution of employees across age groups and education levels.
8. Show the total number of employees within each age group.
9. Show the total number of employees within each education level.
10. Presenet the correlation between employee's education backgrounds and their performance ratings.
11. Compare salaries across different education levels for both genders to identify any.
12. Present how the age correlate with the salary for employees in each department.

---

## 📝 Methodology
- **Step 1:** Import & clean data → handle missing values, standardize formats.  
- **Step 2:** Create calculated fields for revenue, profit margins, and growth rates.  
- **Step 3:** Build interactive charts → bar charts, line charts, heatmaps, maps.  
- **Step 4:** Combine charts into dashboards to answer business questions.  
- **Step 5:** Use filters and parameters to allow dynamic user exploration.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Operations department turned out to be the one who has the done most hiring as well as terminating employees showing it is a key department and requires great skillset to sustain.
- Male gender dominates in terms of number of employees in the company.
- Employees who have done only high school are seen to have "needs improvement" performance compared to employees who have done PHD and having "Excellent" performance showcasing the importance of Education and how more educated employees can benefit the company.
- Majority number of employees in the company are the ones who have completed their Bachelors and started working thereafter.
